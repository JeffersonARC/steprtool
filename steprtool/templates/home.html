Welcome to the Remote Operation Portal

page is the portal for operators who are using the club radios remotely. If this is your first visit, we recommend enabling browser notifications. This will notify you when the antennas are disconnected and reconnected by our Lightning Rig Saver. We ask for ID because to show active users at the top right of this screen. You can also see a list of recent activities, such as operators coming and going, antennas

Please check the Chat room and Calendar to see if anyone is already using a radio before you start using it.

The buttons below take you different resources. The left two are for Chat and Calendar. They open that resource in a new tab.

The IC-7300 button opens in a new tab a page running POTACAT. If you are not familiar with POTACAT, you should spend some time getting familiar with it in your own shack. It is free to download and use. You must first login to use it.

From here you can access the various tools and resources available to you, including the chat room, calendar, and control pages for the radios and antennas. You can also see recent activity on the system, such as who is currently operating and any recent changes or updates. If you have any questions or need assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out to the club officers or other operators in the chat room. Happy operating!
